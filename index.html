Our Mission

Ocean Plastics... the world's worst invasive species. Small steps lead to big change. See what you can do to fight the influx of plastics in our oceans.

At Wave Of Change, we are commited to restoring the health of our oceans by tackling the plastics pollution crisis and educating our community on steps that they can enact to help. Our mission is to combine innovative solutions and community support all across the globe to inspire individuals, businesses, and communities to take action and work together to protect our plant's most vital resource: the ocean.

The Wave Of Change community has already made significant strides to fight against plastic pollution. To date we have conducted 27 events across the United States. Removing a total of 17.5 tons of plastics found from beaches, rivers, lakes, wetlands, and local waterways. We have had help from over 300 dedicated volunteers working together to create a healthier enviornment for future generations.
